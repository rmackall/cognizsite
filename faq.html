What happens if I change my mind about my site in the middle of production?

This will probably happen! That's why we stay in constant contact with you and send you updates ALL the time to make sure we are all on the same page.

Do I need to pay the full amount before we start?

Before we start production, we establish a timeline and set milestones for the project. Once those are agreed upon, we sign a contract and collect $299 up front. The remaining payment is due when site it completed.

Do you host the site and set that up or am I responsible for that?

We have a company that we work with often for our hosting. If you would like us to set that up, we do have an additional fee of $150 but we can make sure that you are up and running!

My site is up, looks great and now I need to change something?

We offer one free content update within 30 days of completion. If your site will need continuous updates, we have hourly rates available or even a reduced monthly retainer.
